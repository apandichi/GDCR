Hosting Guide

The Global Day of Coderetreat has become a major, worldwide software development event. As an organizer or facilitator of a coderetreat, you will help thousands of developers to improve their software development skills (which in turn will help to make the world a better place).

Below you will find important information about the Global Day of Coderetreat and resources you can use to help organize your event. We regularly add new content so be sure to check back often!

Fill out the Location Registration Form

If you haven't done so already, please make sure you have filled out the location registration form. This form will help us keep track of contact information, who is hosting what city, etc. After you've filled out the form, we will give you access to an announcements mailing list and optionally a discussion mailing list where you can collaborate with other GDCR hosts and facilitators.

Register your event on the coderetreat.org website

To help advertise your event, you can register it on coderetreat.org. See the answer to How do I list my event on coderetreat.org? in the Hosting FAQ for details.
