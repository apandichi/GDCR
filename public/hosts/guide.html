Hosting Guide

The Global Day of Coderetreat has become a major worldwide software development event. As an organizer or facilitator of a coderetreat, you a part of help thousands of developers to improve their software development skills (which, in turn, will help to make the world a better place).

Below you will find important information about the Global Day of Coderetreat: everything you need in order to register, promote, and organize a great event. We regularly add new content so be sure to check back often!

Register your location

If you haven't done so already, please fill out the location registration form. This form helps us track contact information and GDCR locations. After you've filled out the form, you will receive access to the announcements mailing list and the discussion mailing list where you can collaborate with other GDCR hosts and facilitators.

Promote your event

To help advertise, you can list your event on coderetreat.org. For more details, see the answer to How do I list my event on coderetreat.org? in the Hosting FAQ.

Find local sponsors

As a host, you are responsible for securing sponsors to cover:

  • The event location
  • Food (at least lunch, but preferable coffee in the morning, as well)

If you are having trouble finding sponsors, let us know so we can help you. More information about local sponsors is available in the Hosting FAQ.

Find global sponsors

We are approaching larger companies about global sponsorship again this year. Global sponsorsip funds will be used to cover administrative costs and promotional materials. The remaining funds will be distributed by the Coderetreat Community Contribution Fund (c3f) to aid other organizations in trainng the next generation of software developers.

Feel free to share our Global Sponsor Prospectus with companies you think may be interested in becoming a global sponsor for GDCR. Direct companies to contact the global coordinator team for more information.

Recruit a facilitator

All locations will need a facilitator for the day. Please make sure your facilitator has signed up for the announcement mailing list and/or the discussion mailing list. Both lists are for hosts and facilitators so that everyone will have access to all information and plans.

New to facilitating? Have questions? Stay tuned. We will schedule some facilitator trainings soon.

Enter the About Page Contest

The coderetreat about page needs some help so we've launched the Coderetreat About Page Contest! Encourage local web designers to participate or enter the contest yourself! The winning entry will replace the existing coderetreat about page and the winner will also get two free ebooks. The contest ends on September 30th.

Help the global coordinators

We are looking for volunteers to help us coordinate the global event. For example, I'm sure you will all agree gdcr.coderetreat.org could use some work. We are looking for volunteers to help with:

  • designing and managing the websites
  • assisting with training new hosts and facilitators
  • creating promotional materials and templates for use by local event hosts
  • helping with general event record-keeping and tracking

If you would like to take-it-up-a-notch by helping with any of the above, or if you know someone who might be able to help, please email us! Together, we can make this event more amazing!
