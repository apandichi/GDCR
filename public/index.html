Welcome to the Global Day of Coderetreat! December 8rd, 2012

What is it?

Global Day of Coderetreat is a world-wide event celebrating passion and software craftsmanship. Over 2200 passionate software developers in 90 cities around the world will spend the day practicing the craft of software development using the coderetreat format. You can find out more information about the day on the 2012 global day announcement.

Where is it?

Last year, we had 94 cities around the world. This year, we are aiming for 200! We are currently in the process of registering cities. If you are interested in hosting an event in your city. Please fill in the host form. As we register events, you can find out if there is one in your city on the official GDCR2012 event list page.

Where will Corey be?

In order to raise awareness, Corey Haines will repeat his time-traveling feat from last year. He will facilitate the first coderetreat in Sydney, Australia, then hop a flight that lands in Honolulu, Hawaii, at in the morning of the same day. He'll then facilitate the last coderetreat of the day.
