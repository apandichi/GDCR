Welcome to the Global Day of Coderetreat! December 14th, 2013

The Global Day of Coderetreat is going on now! Checkout live.coderetreat.org to see the current status of every coderetreat. The GDCR 2013 Timeline (PDF) shows when each local coderetreat will take place.

What is it?

The Global Day of Coderetreat is a world-wide event celebrating passion and software craftsmanship. Last year, over 2000 passionate software developers in 150 cities around the world spent the day practicing the craft of software development using the coderetreat format. This year, our goal is to have 200 participating cities! With an average of 20-25 participants per city, that's over 3000 developers! You can find out more information about the day on the 2013 global day announcement.

Where is it?

Coderetreats are being held in more than 165 locations worldwide! See the map below and the official list of events.

Who is organizing this?

Global Day of Coderetreat is an event organized by the Coderetreat Community Contribution Fund. Main organizers are Jim Hurne, Alissa Conaty, Adi Bolboaca, and Martin Klose.
